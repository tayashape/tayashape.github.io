Pancakes With Raisins

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 2 eggs
  • half of cup raisins
  • butter
  • forth of a cup milk
  • Sugar

Recipe

  1. flour mix with eggs,butter,milk,sugar.
  2. Don't forget to rinse the raisins before mixing it.
  3. When you are done with that you should get a pan.
  4. When the pan is on the stove make little pancakes out of you're hands then put it on the pan.
  5. Then when you are done with that you should cook on each side for 3 minutes and flip when 3 minutes has past.
